Directions

By Boat

There are many ways to travel to Martha's Vineyard, the easiest is likely by taking the steamship from Woods Hole, MA. There are several other ferry services available, summarized here.

By Airplane

There is a commercial airport located on Martha's Vineyard (MVY). Buses and car rentals are available from the airport.

By Car

While an island, it is possible to drive to Martha's Vineyard by making a reservation with the steamship authority. Parking will be available at the wedding for those driving.

Warning! Reservations to take a car from Woods Hole to Martha's Vineyard can fill up months in advance.
